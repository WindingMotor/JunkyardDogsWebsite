Meet Continuity!

Features

Swerve Drive

Our swerve drive system provides the robot the capability to effortlessly travel in any direction, providing unparalleled maneuverability on the field.

Elevator

A double stage lightweight aluminum elevator capable of bringing game pieces up to 6ft for scoring reliably. Has a zero failure rate!

Grabber

A small and complex arm, with wheels and attached to the elevator, that can change its angle to align game pieces with their targets.
