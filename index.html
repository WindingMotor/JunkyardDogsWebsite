About Us

We are team 2106 the Junkyard Dogs of Goochland High school. Our team was founded 16 years ago in 2007. Since then we have had multiple generations of students devoted to developing skills such as engineering, programming, teamwork, enthusiasm, and much more. Through practices and competitions students learn these necessary skills needed to excel at their future lives and jobs.

FIRST Charged Up!

The field is littered with cubes and cones. Robots race to obtain these objects and place them onto their respective spots to earn points. Cones are placed upright onto rods and cubes are placed on platforms. As time runs out robots head to the charge station, a large platform that moves with weight, robots must balance themselves to earn the endgame points.
