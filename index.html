About Our Team

We are the Junkyard Dogs, Team 2106 of Goochland High School, a passionate and dedicated robotics team that competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition. Our team's mission is to inspire and educate students' minds about the exciting world of robotics, engineering, communication, and more!

With a strong focus on innovation and teamwork, we strive to push the boundaries of what is possible each year in our competitions and community. Our mentors build strong relationships with students as they learn about a variety of topics like design, machining, electronics, business, networking, teamwork, and more!
