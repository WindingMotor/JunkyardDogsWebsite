About Us

We are team 2106 the Junkyard Dogs of Goochland High school. Our team was founded 16 years ago in 2007. Since then we have had multiple generations of students devoted to developing skills such as engineering, programming, teamwork, enthusiasm, and much more. Through practices and competitions students learn these necessary skills needed to excel at their future lives and jobs.
