About Us

We are FRC Team 2106, the Junkyard Dogs of Goochland High School. Founded in 2006, our team has thrived for 16 years, fostering crucial skills in engineering, programming, teamwork, and more. Through practice and competition, our students learn to excel in their future careers and personal lives.

What is FIRST?

FIRST is a global organization that strives to motivate young individuals to become future innovators and leaders in science and technology. With their robotics programs, FIRST offers students the opportunity to gain practical experience in engineering, programming, and critical thinking, while fostering teamwork, leadership, and communication abilities. FIRST has made a significant impact on thousands of students worldwide by encouraging them to pursue STEM fields and reach their full potential.

Who do we look for?

Students who embrace the spirit of learning with enthusiasm and passion. Whether your interests lie in designing mechanical systems or connecting with the community, robotics has many different roles and learning experiences for you to explore and engage in!

Team Members

Our team is composed of 18 students and 4 incredible mentors, each with substantial industry experience. As our team grows we look for more diverse and eager students to join us as well as new mentors.
