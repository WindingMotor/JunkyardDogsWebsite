Meet Maestro, Our 2024 Robot

The Game : Crescendo

Alliances of three teams compete to score notes, 14in hollow donut shaped dics, in various field elements, amplify their speaker, harmonize, and take the spotlight within a time limit. Additional rewards are given for meeting scoring thresholds and cooperating with opponents. Robots operate autonomously for the first fifteen seconds and then under driver control for the remaining two minutes and fifteen seconds. The game involves playing notes in an amp, amplifying the speaker, and earning points for cooperation. Bonus points are awarded for harmony and spotlighting robots on stage. The alliance with the highest points at the end wins.
